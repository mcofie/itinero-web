# Embracing Solitude

Solo travel is one of the most liberating experiences you can have. It forces you to step out of your comfort zone and truly engage with your surroundings.

## Safety First

When you're on your own, safety is paramount.
*   **Share your location**: Always let someone know where you are.
*   **Trust your gut**: If a situation feels off, leave.
*   **Use Itinero**: Our verified guides can be a safety net in a new city.

## Making Connections

Hostels, walking tours, and local meetups are great ways to meet fellow travelers. Don't be afraid to say hello!
